How to submit a paper
  • Click Submit a Paper ↗ above and paste an arXiv ID (e.g. 2301.12345) or the full abstract URL.
  • Add a short note on why you find it interesting — it helps everyone prepare.
  • You don’t need to present — just submit something you’d like to discuss.
  • Students at every level are welcome and encouraged to submit!
  • Submissions for the current week close at end of Sunday; approved papers appear here within a minute.
